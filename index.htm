Bots 'n Bolts™ for Android™ and Java™

Classic platform gameplay with retro graphics

Bots 'n Bolts is a traditional platformer inspired by 8-bit and 16-bit games from the '80s and '90s.
It's free to download and play with no in-app purchases and no ads.

Play as Void, an advanced robot designed by Dr. Root, to fight Dr. Final's Array of fiendish robots.
Run, jump, and fire your arm cannon through twelve exciting levels!
Collect upgrade Bolts to earn new abilities.
Defend our world, and save the day!
